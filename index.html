Contract Overview

BNBRoyal Smart Contract is a fork of the open-sourced smart contract by BNBFactor.

However, the original smart contract is vulnerable of high value withdrawals.

To fix and fool-proof this exploit, the team has added a new feature: Anti-Whale.

Feature: Anti-Whale

Users with high investment value can have high returns in less than a day.

Because BNBRoyal offers high APR yields, an example of a whale investing 300 BNB can accumulate 24 BNB in 24 hours (in Plan 4 with 8% daily).

To combat this exploit, we have added a few simple tweaks to the contract.


These few lines limit users to be able to withdraw a maximum of 20 BNB per 24 hours.


Wahle investors will only be able to withdraw — 20 BNB MAX — and the excess BNB will be sent back to his BNBRoyal Account. The excess BNB can then be withdrawn on the next available withdraw time (+24 hours).
